About Us

HoopWithSean is a transformative platform dedicated to empowering disadvantaged communities through the art of basketball. Rooted in my own journey, it offers a beacon of hope and guidance. Drawing from personal experiences, I provide tailored advice and insights to inspire individuals to embrace basketball not just as a sport, but as a pathway to personal growth and community engagement. With a focus on inclusivity and mentorship, HoopWithSean fosters resilience, teamwork, and self-belief. Together, we break barriers, defy odds, and forge a brighter future. Join us on the court, where every hoop is a step towards empowerment and every dribble, a testament to potential.

Sean's Advice

For Everyone:

Advice 1: It's all about TURNOVERS

A lot of players even NBA players has the problem of having turnovers, so does everyone else. Most people only focus on how to score a 50-piece, when they turn the ball over more than 50 points worth of position. Learn how to protect your ball, how to make good passes, how to catch ball while sprinting and not turnover, and more. If you can play a whole game of basketball with 0 turnover, good job, you are already on top of so many people.

Advice 2: Make free throws before 3 pointers

Who doesn't want to be Stephen Curry, right? But hey, all you see on TV is him after endless hours of shots training for YEARS! If you notice, his free throw percentage is insanely high. Focus on a smooth looking textbook-kinda shooting form first, then start using it to shoot every day from close to the rim to free throw then to 3 pointers. One thing I always like saying is that "If you have to push your arms to shoot, you are not shooting, you are throwing so take one step closer and retry".
